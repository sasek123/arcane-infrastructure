06 — Brand Rules
Do
Arcane uppercase, Syne 800 — always the dominant element
Dept tag in Space Mono, Parchment, with the line prefix mark
Parchment as the single accent — sparingly, purposefully
Use weight contrast: 800 display vs 400 body creates tension
Ghost text and negative space as compositional tools
Don't
Never abbreviate — not AE, not AI, not Arc
Dept name never larger, heavier, or more prominent than parent
No gradients, glows, textures, or drop shadows
Don't mix Equity and Infrastructure in one asset
Parchment never on light or white backgrounds
